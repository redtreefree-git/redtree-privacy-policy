Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

RedTree ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and handled when you use the RedTree mobile application.

Camera Access

The RedTree app may request access to your device’s camera.

Why we use the camera:

  • To allow users to capture photos within the app
  • To support features that require image input (such as profile photos or content uploads)

We do not access the camera without your explicit permission.

How We Use Information

  • Images captured using the camera are used only for app functionality
  • We do not sell, rent, or share camera data with third parties
  • We do not use camera data for advertising or tracking

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Third-Party Services

RedTree may use trusted third-party services (such as cloud infrastructure) to operate the app. These services are required to follow appropriate data protection practices.

User Control

You may deny or revoke camera permission at any time through your device settings. Some features may not function correctly without camera access.

Children’s Privacy

RedTree is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
